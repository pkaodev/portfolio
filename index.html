About me

I am a Swedish citizen who has lived in the UK for over 20 years. Before I started my career as a developer I worked as a chemistry researcher and science teacher.

I am currently working as a Junior Software Developer and Mentor at Northcoders.

Whilst most of my projects so far have been JavaScript based full-stack web apps, I have a wide range of interests in all things tech and internet related, and I am always looking to learn new skills and challenge myself.

When I am not developing I enjoy cooking and baking dishes from around the world, and am a keen board/video gamer.
